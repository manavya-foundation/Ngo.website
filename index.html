About Us

Manavya Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping underprivileged communities through education support, healthcare awareness, and social welfare programs.To understand community development programs aimed at promoting environmental sustainability, sanitation, access to clean water, Women empowerment, child welfare,and inclusion of person with disabilities. design and implement transformative social interventions aimed at improving quality of life, enhancing dignity,and fostering sustainable development among economically weaker sections of society.To promote holistic well-being of vulnerable and marginalized communities by undertaking initiatives in the fields of education, healthcare, livelihood development, nutrition, sanitation, mental health, and social welfare. To design and implement transformative social interventions aimed at improving quality of life, enhancing dignity, and fostering sustainable development among economically weaker sections of society. To advance education by establishing, supporting, or aiding schools, learning centers, skill development institutes, vocational training programs, digital literacy initiatives, and scholarships for underprivileged individuals. To promote healthcare and preventive health awareness, including organizing medical camps, mental health programs, community health initiatives, and facilitating access to affordable healthcare services. To support livelihood generation and economic empowerment through skill development, entrepreneurship support.to undertake any other lawful charitable activties incidental or ancillary to the attainment of the above objectives,provided that the same are carried out without profit motive and in accordance with applicable laws.
